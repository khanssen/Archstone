FedRAMP Services

Navigate the FedRAMP Accreditation Process with Confidence

Our FedRAMP advisory services provide expert guidance and support to organizations looking to meet the Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program (FedRAMP) requirements for their information systems. Archstone Security has extensive experience in boundary definition, documentation creation, gap analyses, and policy, procedures, and plan development for FedRAMP compliance.
  • Boundary definition: We can help you identify the scope and boundaries of your system, including all of the components, interfaces, and data flows that fall within the scope of FedRAMP.
  • Documentation creation: We can assist you with the creation of all necessary documentation, including system security plans, risk assessments, contingency plans, and other documents required by FedRAMP.
  • Gap analyses: We can perform a gap analysis to identify any areas where your system may not meet the FedRAMP requirements, and provide recommendations for how to address those gaps.
  • Policy, procedures, and plan development: We can work with you to develop policies, procedures, and plans that ensure your system is compliant with FedRAMP requirements and can be effectively managed and maintained over time.
Our FedRAMP advisory services are tailored to meet the specific needs of your organization and can be delivered on a project basis or as ongoing support. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you achieve FedRAMP compliance.
