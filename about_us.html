Our experience is what sets us apart.

At Archstone Security, we pride ourselves on delivering cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions that truly benefit your organization's security posture and budget. Founder Karen Stanford, a seasoned expert with over two decades of Federal cybersecurity consulting experience, stands as a beacon of expertise and innovation in the industry.

Boasting an impressive track record, Archstone Security, LLC was founded by Karen Stanford, whose distinguished career includes over two decades of Federal cybersecurity consulting for the most discriminating clients. Stanford's accolades include guiding multiple high-profile clients through the intricate FedRAMP JAB accreditation process, and she is celebrated for her extensive expertise in the FedRAMP space. Notably, Stanford's prowess earned her a position as one of the pioneering assessors within the Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification Framework. With a rich history as a former director at the esteemed FedRAMP 3PAO Coalfire, Stanford's influence spans across diverse sectors, having provided invaluable support to renowned cloud service providers, civilian Federal agencies, Department of Defense clients, Offices of the Inspector General, and Federally funded research and development centers. Stanford's proficiency shines in her ability to architect innovative cloud solutions, spanning from cutting-edge technologies to entirely open-source environments, cementing her reputation as a visionary in the field. This wealth of accomplishments positions Archstone Security as a beacon of excellence, ready to optimize your cybersecurity landscape for a safer and more secure digital future.
